Courses we offer

Bachelor of Science

The Bachelor of Science (BSc) is a highly valued undergraduate degree that provides foundational and advanced knowledge in various scientific disciplines. It is designed for students who have completed their 10+2 education in the science stream and are eager to pursue a career in research, academia, healthcare, technology, or industry sectors. This extensive guide covers every aspect of the BSc program—including course details, eligibility, syllabus, specializations, career prospects, salary trends, and further education opportunities—to help you make an informed decision about pursuing this degree.

Bachelor of Computer Application

An online BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) is an undergraduate degree program in computer applications that is delivered entirely online. The program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer programming, database management, software development, and other key areas of computer science. An online BCA is suitable for a range of individuals, including recent high school graduates looking to pursue a career in computer science, working professionals seeking to enhance their skills and knowledge in this field, and entrepreneurs seeking to start or grow their businesses in the technology sector.
